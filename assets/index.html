    • Manny Pacquaio

    Life

    Born on December 17th 1978 in Kibawe Philipines. To provide for his family due to extreme poverty, Manny at the age of 12 with the help of his uncle turned to boxing.

    Career

    Became a proffesional boxer at the age of 16. At the age of 20 he won his first international title by becoming the flyweight champion of the world. Through his career he became the first and only boxer to become a 8 division champion.

    Record

    His record stands at : 72 total fights, 62 wins, 8 losses, 2 draws with 39 KO's
